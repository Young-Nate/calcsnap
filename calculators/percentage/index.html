How to Calculate a Percentage

A percentage is a fraction expressed as a part of 100. The word "percent" means "per hundred." Percentages are used everywhere — from discounts and taxes to statistics and grades.

What is P% of X? Result = (P / 100) × X X is what % of Y? Result = (X / Y) × 100 % Change from A to B? Result = ((B − A) / A) × 100
Example — What is 20% of 150?

Step 1: Convert 20% to decimal → 0.20

Step 2: Multiply → 0.20 × 150 = 30

Answer: 20% of 150 is 30

Example — 45 is what % of 180?

Step 1: Divide → 45 ÷ 180 = 0.25

Step 2: Multiply by 100 → 0.25 × 100 = 25

Answer: 45 is 25% of 180
